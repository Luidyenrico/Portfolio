Problem

Finding the right job in a sea of listings.

Job seekers spend hours manually browsing through hundreds of job listings, trying to find positions that match their specific skill set. The process is time-consuming, repetitive, and often leads to missed opportunities or applications for poorly matched roles.

  • Manual and exhausting job search process
  • Difficulty filtering relevant opportunities
  • No intelligent skill-to-job matching
  • Lack of structured analysis per vacancy

Solution

AI-powered job matching and analysis.

A system built with Python that connects to job listing APIs, filters vacancies based on the user's competencies, and leverages AI to provide a detailed analysis of each matching position — including fit score, required skills breakdown, and actionable recommendations.

  • Automated job search via external APIs
  • Intelligent skill-based filtering and ranking
  • AI-driven vacancy analysis and scoring
  • Personalized recommendations per listing

Tech Stack

Python
OpenAI
APIs

Results

A completely automated pipeline that eliminates the tedious manual job search, delivering only relevant, pre-analyzed opportunities directly to the user.
